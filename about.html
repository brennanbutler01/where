At the WHEREHOUSE, we believe in the power of community*. We believe that who you are and what you do, in large part, is because of who and what you surround yourself with. It’s our mission to provide our community with the best of the best-- in retail, food, art, and more. We hope, through constant celebration of what our city has to offer, that we are all more inspired, vibrant, and creative as a result.

--

*Community: a feeling of fellowship with others, as a result of sharing common attitudes, interests, and goals. The WHEREHOUSE community is strictly inclusive. Otherwise, it’s not a community at all. The WHEREHOUSE is a safe space for everyone. We insist on anti-racism, anti-sexism and anti-homophobia as well as general respect towards all of our family members. If you’re part of us, you’re supporting all of us.

Learn more about our pledge to uplift disenfranchised voices here: thewhere.house/community
