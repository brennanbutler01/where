COMMUNITY: A FEELING OF FELLOWSHIP WITH OTHERS, AS A RESULT OF SHARING COMMON ATTITUDES, INTERESTS, AND GOALS. THE WHEREHOUSE COMMUNITY IS STRICTLY INCLUSIVE. OTHERWISE, IT’S NOT A COMMUNITY AT ALL. THE WHEREHOUSE IS A SAFE SPACE FOR EVERYONE. WE INSIST ON ANTI-RACISM, ANTI-SEXISM AND ANTI-HOMOPHOBIA AS WELL AS GENERAL RESPECT TOWARDS ALL OF OUR FAMILY MEMBERS. IF YOU’RE PART OF US, YOU’RE SUPPORTING ALL OF US.

Where we support:

ACLU Southern California: The American Civil Liberties Union (ACLU) defends the fundamental rights outlined in the United States Constitution and the Bill of Rights. These include the right to freedom of speech and assembly, the right to religious freedom, due process of law, equality before the law and the right to privacy.

Donate: https://action.aclu.org/give/support-aclu-socal?ms_aff=CS&initms_aff=CS&ms_chan=web&initms_chan=web

VOTE SAVE AMERICA: Vote Save America is here to be your one-stop-shop for all the information and tools you need to participate in 2020. You’ve made it this far. You’re crushing it. Now what else can you do? The answer is MORE.

Donate: https://secure.actblue.com/donate/getmitch

BLACK LIVES MATTER LA: #BlackLivesMatter was founded in 2013 in response to the acquittal of Trayvon Martin’s murderer. Black Lives Matter Foundation, Inc is a global organization in the US, UK, and Canada, whose mission is to eradicate white supremacy and build local power to intervene in violence inflicted on Black communities by the state and vigilantes.

Donate: https://secure.actblue.com/donate/ms_blm_homepage_2019

EQUAL JUSTICE INITIATIVE: The Equal Justice Initiative is committed to ending mass incarceration and excessive punishment in the United States, to challenging racial and economic injustice, and to protecting basic human rights for the most vulnerable people in American society.

Donate: https://support.eji.org/give/153413/#!/donation/checkout

LOS ANGELES LGBT CENTER: Since 1969 the Los Angeles LGBT Center has cared for, championed, and celebrated LGBT individuals and families in Los Angeles and beyond. They are an unstoppable force in the fight against bigotry and the struggle to build a better world, a world in which LGBT people can be healthy, equal, and complete members of society.

Donate: https://donate.lalgbtcenter.org/secure/help-build-world-where-lgbt-people-thrive

THE WOMENS CENTER FOR CREATIVE WORK: Women’s Center for Creative Work cultivates L.A.’s feminist creative communities and practices.

Donate: https://donate.womenscenterforcreativework.com

TURN AROUND ARTS CA: Turnaround Arts: California is a nonprofit organization that administers the Turnaround Arts program statewide to engage, empower, and transform the state’s highest-need schools and communities through the arts.

Donate: https://turnaroundartsca.org/donate/
